Intro

Hello. My name's Joshua, and welcome to my introduction. My first experience with programming was in high school when I took a computer programming class on a whim. I immediately fell in love, designing awful trivia games before progressing to awful RPG games. I wasn't aware at the time just how deep the river ran and would spend my twenties being unaware of aforementioned depth. Lambda School offered me a chance to reconnect with my teenage-self to continue my exploration into the wonderful world of coding. I'm hoping that whoever is reading this will be the one to offer me my next opportunity to seek out new experiences in coding.

By the way, check out my awesome work.
